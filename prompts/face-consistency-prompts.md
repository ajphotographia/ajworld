# Face Consistency Prompts

This file contains prompt structures for keeping the same facial identity across AI-generated images.

## Strong Face Consistency Prompt

Use the uploaded face image as the strict identity reference. Preserve the exact same facial identity with maximum accuracy.

Keep the same:
- Face shape
- Jawline
- Cheekbones
- Forehead proportions
- Eyebrow shape
- Eye shape
- Nose shape
- Lip shape
- Chin shape
- Skin tone
- Skin texture
- Hairstyle
- Facial hair
- Age appearance
- Natural facial proportions

Do not beautify, redesign, stylize, replace, or reinterpret the face. The final image must clearly look like the same real person from the uploaded reference image.

## Negative Instructions

Do not change identity.
Do not make the face slimmer.
Do not change the nose.
Do not change the eyebrows.
Do not change the hairstyle.
Do not make the subject look younger or older.
Do not create a similar-looking person.
Do not make the face cartoonish.
Do not over-smooth skin texture.
