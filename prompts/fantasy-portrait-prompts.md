# Fantasy Portrait Prompts

This file contains cinematic fantasy portrait prompt examples for AI creators.

## Dreamy Fantasy Portrait Prompt

Create an ultra-realistic cinematic fantasy portrait of the uploaded subject, preserving the exact same facial identity from the reference image.

The subject is standing in a dreamy fantasy world with soft glowing light, magical atmosphere, floating particles, gentle fog, and cinematic depth of field.

Style:
- Ultra-realistic
- High detail
- Cinematic lighting
- Natural skin texture
- Sharp facial details
- Dreamy fantasy mood
- Soft emotional expression
- Professional photography look

Environment:
- Magical fantasy landscape
- Soft morning light
- Floating petals
- Light fog
- Dreamy background
- Elegant composition

Camera:
- 9:16 vertical
- Portrait framing
- Shallow depth of field
- Subject in sharp focus
- Background softly blurred

Identity instruction:
The face must remain exactly the same as the uploaded reference image. Do not change facial structure, skin tone, hairstyle, or natural proportions.
