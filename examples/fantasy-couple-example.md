# Fantasy Couple Portrait Example

## Purpose

This example prompt is for creating a cinematic fantasy couple portrait while preserving the faces from uploaded reference images.

## Prompt

Use Image A as the strict facial identity reference for the woman and Image B as the strict facial identity reference for the man.

Preserve both identities with maximum accuracy. The final image must clearly look like the same real woman from Image A and the same real man from Image B, not similar-looking people, not beautified versions, and not reinterpreted characters.

For the woman, preserve her exact face shape, jawline, cheekbones, forehead proportions, eyebrow shape, eye shape, nose shape, lip shape, chin shape, skin tone, hairstyle, and natural facial proportions.

For the man, preserve his exact face shape, jawline, cheekbones, forehead proportions, eyebrow shape, eye shape, nose shape, lips, chin, beard style, skin tone, hairstyle, and natural facial proportions.

Create an ultra-realistic cinematic fantasy couple portrait in a dreamy magical environment. The couple is standing close together in an emotional romantic pose. Add soft fog, glowing particles, magical atmosphere, cinematic lighting, natural skin texture, high detail, sharp focus, and professional fantasy photography style.

The image should feel emotional, dreamy, magical, and cinematic.

Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical.

## Negative Prompt

No face change.
No identity change.
No cartoon look.
No plastic skin.
No extra fingers.
No distorted hands.
No bad anatomy.
No wrong face shape.
No changed hairstyle.
No artificial expression.
No over-smoothed skin.
No unrealistic proportions.
